How It Works

Your Time On Set.

Same team, same materials, same safety standard as our flagship Doors Off day — condensed into a sharper, faster session built for anyone curious about the craft.

You'll walk in not knowing how a screen explosion actually works. You'll walk out having rigged and detonated one.

01

The Briefing

Coffee, kit, and a quick brief. Your SFX supervisor sets the scene: what you're about to do, every safety protocol, and the craft behind a great on-screen bang.

02

The Theory

An insider's look at how cinematic explosions actually work. Low explosives. Black powder. The principle of confinement — the difference between a movie bang and a real one.

03

Rig The Charge

Under close supervision, you'll build a real black powder charge, wire it to the line, and walk through the final safety checks alongside the team.

04

Action.

3 — 2 — 1. The detonator is yours. The cameras roll. The bang is real. You leave with the footage and the story.
